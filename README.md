# Morsy

**Morsy** is a pre-alpha mobile app that helps users manage food in their fridge and pantry, track expiration dates, organize shopping lists, and reduce food waste.

> **Status:** Pre-alpha  
> **Platform:** Android APK  
> **Source code:** Private  
> **Feedback:** support.morsy@gmail.com

## Download and feedback

Morsy can be downloaded and tested by anyone through the public APK releases available in this repository.

Since the app is currently in **pre-alpha**, bugs, missing features, and unexpected behavior may occur.  
If you find a bug, notice a problem, or have ideas to improve the app, please contact us at:

**support.morsy@gmail.com**

Your feedback helps us improve Morsy before future alpha, beta, and stable releases.

## About Morsy

Morsy is designed to make home food management simpler and more organized.

The app focuses on:

- fridge and pantry inventory
- manual product insertion
- barcode-based product insertion
- expiration date tracking
- shopping list management
- favorites and activity history
- consumption reports
- local expiration notifications
- user accounts
- Base, Medium and Premium plan structure
